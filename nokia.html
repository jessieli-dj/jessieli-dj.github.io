Nokia Internship

I worked closely with a senior interaction designer to create an experience around leveraging automation to help network engineers save time and reduce risk of human error.

The experience was brought to life through the close collaboration of a multidisciplinary team composed of designers, developers, QA, and product manager.

Tools

When

What I Learned

Using judgment to balance desireability, viability and feasibility

I applied design thinking to create designs that provide clear value for the user and business, and communicated consistently with the product team to stay updated on technical limitations.

Communicating for successful multidisciplinary collaborations

I set expectations frequently and clearly to adapt to changing requirements. This reduced miscommunications and allowed us to manage our timelines effectively.

Adapting to designing for a highly technical domain and user

I took initiative to educate myself on the domain and Nokia's design system using internal and external resources. As a result, I was quickly given greater responsibilities.

Reflection

My internship exceeded my expectations. I can’t wait to take what I’ve learned into the next stage of my career. Thanks to the Nokia ION UX team for a great 8 months!
