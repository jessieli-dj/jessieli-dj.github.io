Nokia Internship

I worked closely with a senior interaction designer to create an experience around leveraging automation to help network engineers save time and reduce risk of human error.

The experience was brought to life through the close collaboration of a multidisciplinary team composed of designers, developers, QA, and product manager.

Role

  • Applied design thinking to create designs that provide clear value for the end-user and business
  • Conducted design reviews regularly as part of an iterative design process which led to a stronger final proposal
  • Communicated with an interdisciplinary team during the implementation phase to clarify and facilitate questions
  • Planned and executed a customer research project in collaboration with a UX researcher
